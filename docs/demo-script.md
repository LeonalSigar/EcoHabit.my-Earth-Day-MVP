# EcoHabit.my Demo Script

## AI Voice Narration

Use this as a single voiceover block:

“This is EcoHabit dot my, an Earth Day web app built around Malaysian daily habits. Instead of another generic carbon dashboard, it gives practical actions people can actually do today. The app includes a personalized prompt, so if I describe my situation, it suggests a realistic next move. I can complete actions from the feed, and the points and streak update instantly. There is also a lightweight community wall where users can post one anonymous pledge, so the app feels social instead of purely personal. The final share card turns that progress into a poster-style export with a QR code back to the live app. The goal is simple: make climate-friendly behavior feel specific, local, and easy to repeat.”

## Shorter AI Voice Version

“EcoHabit dot my is an Earth Day app built around Malaysian daily habits. It suggests practical actions, tracks momentum, lets users post a simple pledge, and ends with a shareable story card. The goal is to make climate action feel local, realistic, and repeatable.”

## Video Flow

### 45-60 second version

1. Open the live app: `https://eco-habit-my-earth-day-mvp.vercel.app`
2. Show the welcome modal for 1-2 seconds
3. Dismiss the modal
4. Pause on the hero image and score area
5. Enter this prompt:
   `I'm going to lunch in PJ and taking the MRT later`
6. Show the personalized suggestion
7. Complete 2-3 actions from the feed
8. Pause briefly on the points and streak update
9. Scroll to the pledge composer and post a short pledge
10. Show the community wall for 2-3 seconds
11. End on the share card with the QR code visible

## Shot-by-Shot Timing

1. `0:00-0:04` Welcome modal and first impression
2. `0:04-0:10` Hero section and Malaysian framing
3. `0:10-0:18` Personalized prompt input and suggestion
4. `0:18-0:28` Complete actions and show score/streak movement
5. `0:28-0:38` Community pledge wall and one new pledge
6. `0:38-0:50` Share card, QR code, and final hold

## Recording Tips

- Keep the layout at phone width if it looks stronger on video
- Move slowly enough that the UI feels intentional
- End with the QR code and app URL visible for at least 2 seconds
- If using AI voice, leave a little silence between scenes so the cuts feel cleaner
