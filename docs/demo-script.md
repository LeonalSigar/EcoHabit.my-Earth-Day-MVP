# EcoHabit.my Demo Script

## 45-Second Spoken Script

Start on the welcome modal and hero:

“This is EcoHabit.my, a Malaysia-flavoured Earth Day app built around habits that already fit daily life here.”

Dismiss the modal and show the main view:

“Instead of another generic carbon dashboard, it gives practical actions around commuting, food, reusables, energy, and waste.”

Use the personalized prompt or tap an action:

“If I describe my situation, the app suggests a realistic next move. I can also complete actions directly from the feed.”

Complete 2 or 3 actions:

“As actions are completed, points and streak update instantly. The local context is built into the labels, examples, and action language.”

Scroll to the pledge wall:

“There is also a lightweight community layer. Users can post one pledge, and the wall is pre-seeded so it never feels empty.”

End on the share card:

“The final moment is the poster-style share card with a QR code back to the live app. The goal is to make climate action feel specific, local, and easy to repeat.”

## Video Flow

1. Open the live app: `https://eco-habit-my-earth-day-mvp.vercel.app`
2. Show the welcome modal for 1-2 seconds, then dismiss it
3. Pause on the hero image and header
4. Enter a short personalized prompt such as:
   `I'm going to lunch in PJ and taking the MRT later`
5. Show the suggested action result
6. Mark 2-3 actions as completed
7. Pause briefly on the points and streak update
8. Scroll to the pledge composer and post a short pledge
9. Show the community wall for social proof
10. End on the share card with the QR code visible

## Recording Tips

- Keep the whole video under 45-60 seconds
- Use the phone-width layout if it feels stronger on camera
- Keep the cursor movement slow and deliberate
- End with the QR code and URL visible for 2-3 seconds
